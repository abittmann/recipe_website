Spaghetti Bolognese

A slow simmered, full bodied Italian meat sauce that can be enjoyed on top of tagliatelle pasta or as the main sauce in lasagna ala Bolognese. This true Italian classic Bolognese sauce cannot be made quickly. Allow yourself plenty of time for this outstanding sauce to mature.

Cook time: 2 hours
Difficulty: Easy
Rating: 4.9/5

Step 1

Finely dice your onions, carrots and celery & fry in a pan with some olive oil until golden brown.

Step 2

Add the pancetta to the soffrito. Let the pancetta render down.

Step 3

Add the meat and cook until caramelised.

Step 4

Deglaze by adding the wine. Scrape up all the caramelised bits at the bottom of the pot!
